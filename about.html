Our Story

Building Our Real Estate Empire

White Omar Property Solutions was founded on a simple yet powerful premise: that strategic real estate investment creates generational wealth. What began as a small operation focused on tax lien acquisitions has grown into a diversified real estate investment company with holdings across multiple sectors.

Our founder recognized early on that success in real estate requires more than just capital—it requires expertise, dedication, and a commitment to excellence. Today, we continue building our portfolio through strategic acquisitions, rehabilitations, and property management. While our primary focus is growing our own investments, we also share our expertise with select investors who share our vision.
